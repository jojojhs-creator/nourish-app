# Coffee, Cats & Malak — 30-Day Content Calendar

Channel: Coffee, Cats & Malak ("One girl, three cats, zero peace and quiet ☕😹")
Format: 1 × 15s animated clip per day, Seedance 2.0 FAST, 9:16, 480p

Characters: Malak (human), Mocha (golden Scottish Fold), Sky (white fluffy kitten), Olive (tabby)

**Energy** drives the prompt style (see runbook): **Chaotic** = 3-beat escalating physical
comedy with a big exaggerated reaction payoff. **Cozy** = warm slow moment with one
small comedic surprise at the end.
