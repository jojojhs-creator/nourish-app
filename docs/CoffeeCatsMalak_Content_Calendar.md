# Coffee, Cats & Malak — 30-Day Content Calendar (2 episodes/day)

Channel: Coffee, Cats & Malak ("One girl, three cats, zero peace and quiet ☕😹")
Format: 2 × 15s animated clips per day (Morning + Evening posts), Seedance 2.0 FAST, 9:16, 480p

Characters: Malak (human), Mocha (golden Scottish Fold), Sky (white fluffy kitten), Olive (tabby)

**Energy** drives the prompt style (see runbook): **Chaotic** = escalating physical
comedy with a big exaggerated reaction payoff. **Funny** = deadpan humor and sight
gags with witty comedic timing (no destruction needed). **Cozy** = warm slow moment
with one small comedic surprise at the end. Some episodes are **helper episodes**
where the cats (comically or sweetly) help Malak — keep those heartwarming.

Each scheduled run picks the **next episode not marked Done** — with two runs per day
(morning + evening triggers), episodes are consumed in order, two per day.
