# StoryOwl 60-Day Content Plan

Tracks the bedtime-story episode calendar used by `/storyowl-daily`
(see `docs/STORYOWL_RUNBOOK.md`). Languages alternate EN/AR per the plan below - adjust
freely. Mark an episode `Done` once `/storyowl-daily` has generated and posted it.

| Day | Episode | Language | Title | Premise / Moral | Status |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 1 | EN | Luna the Sleepy Bunny | A bunny who can't fall asleep learns a calming bedtime routine from her forest friends. | Done |
| 2 | 2 | AR | النجمة الصغيرة (The Little Star) | A small star afraid of the dark learns that even small lights matter. | Done |
| 3 | 3 | EN | Theo's Quiet Night | A nervous little hedgehog who fears every nighttime sound is comforted by a tiny firefly friend, learning that even scary sounds can feel cozy with a friend nearby. | Done |
| 4 | 4 | AR | القَمَرُ وَسَامِي (The Moon and Sami) | A little rabbit named Sami can't sleep because he fears the dark, until the moon gently teaches him that darkness is just a warm blanket for the stars. | Done |
| 5 | 5 | EN | _TBD_ | _TBD_ | Not started |

> Add the remaining days (6-60) here, alternating EN/AR, mixing bedtime stories,
> life-lesson stories, and feel-good tales. `/storyowl-daily` picks the first row with
> status `Not started` (or the day number you specify) and fills in the title/premise if
> left as `_TBD_`.
