Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Nourish ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name and email address (for account creation)
  • Body metrics you choose to enter: weight, height, age, and health goals
  • Meal and nutrition data you log in the app
  • Water intake and weight log entries
  • Custom meals and recipes you create

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Nourish app
  • Calculate personalized nutrition goals and recommendations
  • Send meal reminder notifications (only if you enable them)
  • Respond to your support requests

Data Storage

Your personal data is stored locally on your device using secure on-device storage. We do not transmit your meal logs, weight entries, or personal metrics to external servers. Your account credentials are handled securely.

Subscriptions & Payments

Nourish offers optional paid subscriptions processed entirely through Google Play Billing. We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment card information. All billing is managed by Google. You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time through your Google Play account settings.

Camera & Photo Library

The app requests camera access solely for the barcode scanner feature, which allows you to scan packaged food items. We do not store, upload, or transmit any photos or camera data.

Notifications

We may send local push notifications to remind you to log your meals if you enable this feature. These are scheduled entirely on your device and no notification data is sent to our servers.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat — for subscription management. RevenueCat may collect anonymized purchase data. See their privacy policy at revenuecat.com/privacy.
  • Expo / React Native — the app framework. See expo.dev/privacy.

Children's Privacy

Nourish is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by logging out of the app, which removes all locally stored information from your device. To request deletion of any account data, email us at support.nourishlabs@gmail.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
support.nourishlabs@gmail.com
